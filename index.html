ABOUT US

Primefix was founded by a team of experienced professionals who understand the importance of timely, reliable trade work. Through self-performing crews positioned in key markets, we combine nationwide reach with local accountability — so every project is handled by people who take ownership. Whether you’re managing locations across multiple states or coordinating one-off repairs, Primefix makes it simple to get skilled trades on site and the job done right. With presence in major service markets across the U.S., we’re built to move fast and deliver consistently — from first contact to final walkthrough. We show up, we get it done, we stand behind it.
