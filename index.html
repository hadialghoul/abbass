ABOUT US

Primefix is a nationwide trade services vendor built to support multi-site operations with speed, consistency, and accountability. Founded by experienced industry professionals, we understand the expectations that come with vendor programs, brand standards, and time-sensitive work orders.

Through self-performing crews positioned in key markets, we deliver reliable on-site service while maintaining local ownership of every job. Our teams follow strict job site protocols, safety requirements, and client guidelines — ensuring work is completed right the first time.

We currently service major markets including California, Arizona, Texas, Oklahoma, Florida, and Illinois, with scalable coverage to support growing portfolios.

Primefix provides trade services across a wide range of commercial and industrial environments, including schools, retail centers, malls, office buildings, warehouses, and national store locations. From urgent repairs to scheduled maintenance, we make it easy to get qualified technicians on site and projects completed without disruption. We show up, we get it done, we stand behind it.
