Our Services

Expert trade services across electrical, plumbing, HVAC, locksmith, painting, and more.

Electrical

Repairs, fixture replacement, wiring issues, lighting upgrades.

Our certified electricians handle everything from lighting retrofits to complex wiring repairs with precision and safety. We troubleshoot circuits, install new fixtures, and perform panel upgrades to keep your property operating without interruption. Every service meets OSHA standards and local electrical codes, ensuring long-term reliability. Whether it’s routine maintenance or an emergency repair, Primefix delivers fast, professional electrical solutions across commercial and retail spaces.

Plumbing

Leaks, clogs, fixture installs, restroom repairs.

Primefix provides reliable plumbing services to prevent downtime and property damage. Our technicians handle everything from clogged drains and leaking pipes to fixture replacements and full restroom rebuilds. We focus on lasting repairs, not temporary fixes, using quality materials and proven methods. From urgent leak response to preventative maintenance, we deliver clean, professional plumbing solutions that meet commercial standards.

HVAC

Seasonal maintenance, troubleshooting, emergency dispatch.

Our HVAC specialists maintain optimal comfort and efficiency across all types of systems. We handle seasonal maintenance, air filter programs, diagnostics, and emergency service calls with fast turnaround times. Every technician follows manufacturer and EPA standards for safe, efficient operation. Whether it’s a rooftop unit, split system, or thermostat issue, Primefix ensures consistent climate control for your business.

Locksmith

Lock replacements, rekeys, access issues.

Our locksmiths handle commercial lock repairs, replacements, and rekeying for every type of access system. We support property managers and retail clients with reliable door hardware and panic bar installations. With 24/7 availability, we quickly restore access after lockouts or security concerns. Primefix locksmith services combine precision work with professionalism to keep your spaces secure and compliant.

Painting

Interior/exterior touch-ups, full repaint, patch/paint combos.

Primefix painting crews handle projects ranging from single-room touch-ups to full-scale commercial repaints. We work cleanly and efficiently, using durable coatings and finishes designed for high-traffic spaces. Whether it’s a wall repair, color refresh, or complete tenant improvement, our painters ensure professional results with minimal disruption. Every job reflects attention to detail and your brand standards.

Handyman

Doors, ceiling tiles, shelving, signage, mounting.

Our general repair technicians take care of the small but essential jobs that keep your sites running smoothly. We fix doors, patch walls, replace ceiling tiles, and handle basic installations like shelving and signage. Every task is performed safely, efficiently, and on schedule. Primefix handymen arrive prepared, communicate clearly, and leave every workspace neat and complete.

Flooring

Repairs and installations in tile, VCT, LVT, carpet, and more.

Primefix flooring specialists repair and install a wide range of surfaces including tile, vinyl, and carpet. We handle patchwork, transitions, and complete floor replacements with precision and care. Our crews work off-hours when needed to minimize business interruption. From small-area repairs to large commercial renovations, Primefix delivers durable, professional flooring solutions that last.

Janitorial

Deep cleaning, turnover prep, recurring cleans.

We provide dependable janitorial services designed for commercial, retail, and hospitality environments. Our cleaning teams manage daily maintenance, one-time deep cleans, and turnover prep for new tenants. Using professional-grade products and safe practices, we maintain your properties to high standards of cleanliness. Primefix ensures every space is spotless, hygienic, and presentation-ready.

Pest Control

General pest services, one-time or scheduled.

Primefix offers preventative and reactive pest control solutions tailored to your property’s needs. Our licensed specialists handle insects, rodents, and other nuisances quickly and discreetly. We focus on long-term prevention through inspection, exclusion, and safe treatment methods. With flexible scheduling and clear communication, we help protect your business environment year-round.

Pressure Washing

Storefronts, sidewalks, dumpster pads, parking lots.

Our pressure washing services restore the appearance and safety of your exterior spaces. We clean concrete, building facades, and outdoor equipment using professional-grade washers and eco-friendly methods. Regular washing helps prevent buildup, reduce slip hazards, and maintain curb appeal. Primefix delivers spotless, consistent results that reflect the professionalism of your brand.
